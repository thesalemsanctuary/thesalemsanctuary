Why the hell does this exist

We are sentimental freaks over at Uquiz. We want to keep our community alive and nurtured, and it also helps to have all of our things in one place :]


Who are these people?

The population of uquiz...!! we are all really cool trust. Learn more about us in the people and history tabs!


Where can I submit a site change?

Head on over to the "edit site" tab and submit your change! if accurate and suitable, it will be applied to the site shortly.


What is Uquiz?

Its a personality quiz website!! check it out :3


Can I join this group?

OF COURSE! its easy to begin. Head over to the series tab and pick one that looks interesting. click on the title of your chosen series, and it will take you right to the start of one of them. Most of those series post daily and theyre our main source of interaction between each other. Its something of a parasocial relationship but we also have Out-Of-Uquiz exchanges too! (especially in the form of discord, watch parties, and whiteboards!!) we would love to have you.


Whats up with the uquiz elections??

they began around May, i believe! Every month we vote for a new president. Presidents dont do anything special, they just have the ability to create laws. Check out the elections tab for current progress and the history tab for past elections!


What is the office password?

Its locked because Paris (number one Uquiz enemy) would probably spam it if she knew the password. feel free to ask for the password on one of the lacemoss quizzes (and provide a way to contact you so we can give you the password) and we'll get back to you as soon as we can :D
